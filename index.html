About Us

FAQ

Common Questions

What areas do you serve?
We serve Chase, Pritchard, Sorrento, and the broader North Shuswap region in British Columbia. Not sure if you're in our service area? Give us a call at 250-214-0539.
Do you offer free quotes?
Yes. Every estimate is free and comes with no obligation. We'll assess your job and give you a clear, honest price before any work begins — no surprises on your bill.
Are you licensed for gas fitting in BC?
Yes, we hold a valid gas fitting licence for British Columbia and are fully qualified to work on natural gas and propane systems, fireplaces, appliances, and new gas line installations.
What are your business hours?
We're open Monday through Friday, 9:00 am to 5:30 pm. For non-emergency requests outside those hours, use the quote form and we'll follow up the next business day.
What should I do in a plumbing emergency?
Call us immediately at 250-214-0539. For a burst pipe, shut off your main water valve first to limit damage while you wait for help to arrive.
Do you handle both plumbing and HVAC?
Yes. We handle plumbing repairs, heating system installation and service, gas fitting, irrigation, water treatment, and drain cleaning — all through one licensed local company.
Can I request service online?
Yes, use the quote form at the top of this page or the contact form below. You can also call us at 250-214-0539 to speak directly with us.
How long have you been in business?
Our owner and operator Chris has 16 years of industry experience. Duck Range Plumbing and HVAC Ltd. has been serving the Chase and Shuswap community since the beginning — we're your neighbours, not a call centre.
